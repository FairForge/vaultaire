What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files placed on your device by your web browser when you visit a website. They are used to remember your preferences, maintain sessions, and provide security features.

Cookies We Use

stored.ge uses only strictly necessary cookies required for the service to function. We use the following cookies:

Cookie Name Purpose Type Duration
vaultaire_session Maintains your authenticated session after login. HttpOnly, Secure, SameSite=Lax 24 hours
csrf_token Protects against cross-site request forgery attacks using the double-submit cookie pattern. Secure, SameSite=Lax Session (browser close)
flash Displays one-time feedback messages (e.g., "Settings saved") after form submissions. HttpOnly, SameSite=Lax 60 seconds

No Tracking Cookies

stored.ge does not use:

  • Analytics cookies (no Google Analytics, no Plausible, no Matomo).
  • Advertising or remarketing cookies.
  • Third-party cookies of any kind.
  • Fingerprinting or any other cross-site tracking technology.

We do not track your browsing behavior, build advertising profiles, or share any data with ad networks.

Lawful Basis

All cookies used by stored.ge are strictly necessary for the operation of the service (session management, security, and user feedback). Under GDPR and the ePrivacy Directive, strictly necessary cookies do not require consent. Therefore, no cookie consent banner is displayed.

Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling cookies will prevent you from logging in to the stored.ge dashboard, as the session cookie is required for authentication. Clearing cookies will log you out of your current session.
